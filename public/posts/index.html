Presentation_Kemper_Lea

Content presentation is how information (or content) is structured, displayed, and formatted for users in various mediums, such as websites, apps, or printed materials. Focusing on the visual aspects of the content delivery such as design, typography, organization, interactive elements, and media inclusion/placement. Basically how information is shown to you, whether on a website, in an app, or print. A good example of presentation in my everyday use is the dark mode feature on Instagram/Facebook.

Aggregation_Kemper_Lea

Content aggregation is the organization of content from various sources (websites, blogs, and social media) onto a single platform. Allowing users to access a variety of information in one place. An example of content aggregation is Lex 18 my link our local news channel. They have many different articles that are linked on the first page. If you click on the headline it will reroute you to that article. In addition to written content, the page also includes videos, headline stories, and a link to watch the live news show.
