management_bryant_henry

What is Content Management? In Flying Squirrel Glossary, author Deane Barker defines content management as a “wide-ranging” term that refers to the “practice of defining, creating, organizing, securing, reviewing, approving, publishing, and delivering content” (Barker, 2016). In other words, this means that content management is the process of working with and processing the content that a user sees on a particular platform before the user gets to see the content.
