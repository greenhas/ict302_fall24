CMS_bryant_henry

What is a Content Management System? In Web Content Management, author Deane Barker defines a content management system (CMS) as a “software system that automates the tasks around the management of content”. CMSs help content editors with “controlling, securing, and ultimately delivering content” (Barker, 2016). In other words, this means that a content management system is a technology that allows a webmaster, website creator, or website administrator to create content and display it to users.
