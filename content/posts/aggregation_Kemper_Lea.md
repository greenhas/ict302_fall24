Content aggregation is the organization of content from various sources (websites, blogs, and social media) onto a single platform. Allowing users to access a variety of information in one place. An example of content aggregation is Lex 18 [my link](https://www.lex18.com/) our local news channel. They have many different articles that are linked on the first page. If you click on the headline it will reroute you to that article. In addition to written content, the page also includes videos, headline stories, and a link to watch the live news show. This blend of multimedia makes their site easily navigable, making to so you don't have to visit multiple sites to get up to date news.
